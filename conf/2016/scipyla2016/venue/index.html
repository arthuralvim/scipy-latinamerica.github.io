SciPyLA 2016 Venue

SciPyLA 2016 will be held at the Federal University of Santa Catarina campus in Florianópolis, Santa Catarina, Brazil.

The university is located in the heart of Florianópolis, allowing easy access to the city's renowned beaches, parks, museums, and restaurants.
