Damián Avila¶

Personal Home Page: http://www.damian.oquanta.info/
Company: Continuum Analytics
@@damian_avila

﻿Background
Damián Avila is a Software Developer, Data Scientist, Quantitative Analyst/Developer and, formerly, a Biochemist-Immunologist from the U.N.L.P, Buenos Aires, Argentina.
His main focus of interests are Data Science, Finance, Data Visualization and the Jupyter/IPython ecosystem. He uses Python as his main computational tool, but he also has experience working with other high level programming languages.
In the open source area, he has made meaningful contribution to several project, and now he is recognized as a core developer of popular projects, such as Jupyter/IPython, Nikola and Bokeh. He has also started his own projects, such as the "IPython plugin for NINJA-IDE", vIPer and RISE (aka, RJSE, a "live" slideshow machinery for the Jupyter/IPython notebook).
He has presented talks, tutorials and posters in several national and international conferences and he has also written and taught tutorials about the Scientific Python ecosystem.
Finally, he's a member of Python Argentina, Scientific Python Argentina and the Quantitative Finance Club. Currently, he's working as a Software Developer at Continuum Analytics.
