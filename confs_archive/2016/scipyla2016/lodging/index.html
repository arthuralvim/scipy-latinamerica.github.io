The Slaviero hotel has an offer for SciPyLA 2016 Conference attendees. Below follow the prices of each option. On the website of the company, the prices may vary and may be more expensive or economical than that they are on this page, so please check that page before.

LUXURY – R$ 219,00 single / R$ 249,00 double + 2,5% ISS*, breakfast included.

LUXURY EXECUTIVE – R$ 249,00 single / R$ 279,00 double / R$ 309,00 triple + 2,5% ISS*, breakfast included.

To guarantee the reservation, the hotel request data from a valid credit card (number, validity and security code or advance deposit (Bradesco Bank) of 1 daily + ISS*, with balance payable at check-in.
